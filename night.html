Night Tours

Experience the Glen After Dark—Where Nature Whispers Back

Join our exclusive Elder Glen Night Tours, where the forest comes alive in ways few have witnessed. Led by experienced rangers, these guided walks offer a rare chance to explore the park’s breathtaking nocturnal beauty—while uncovering the mysteries that make Elder Glen unlike any other place on Earth.

Tour Options

Whispering Pines Night Hike (Beginner-Friendly – 1.5 miles, 1.5 hours)

  • A serene yet immersive journey through moonlit trails.
  • Listen for the “whispering” pines, a natural phenomenon… or so they say.
  • Learn about the parks nocturnal flora and fauna

The Lantern Walk (Historic & Folklore Tour – 2 miles, 2 hours)

  • Walk through forgotten groves with only lanterns to light the way.
  • Hear the stories of those who walked these trails before us—and those who never left.
  • Sometimes, visitors report seeing old footprints appear in the dirt behind them… though no one was there before.

Veilwood Excursion (Advanced – 3 miles, 3 hours – Adults Only)

  • A deeper trek into the Veilwood, a part of the park only accessible at night.
  • Experience the “Divines”, where time and space feel… different.
  • Special ranger-led sensory experience: Stand in complete silence and darkness for one minute. Perhaps you'll be the lucky one.
  • Signed liability waivers required. Tour may be canceled without explanation.

Important Notes & Safety Guidelines

  • All tours require reservations and fill up quickly.
  • Stay with your group—wandering off is not permitted.
  • If you see lights deep in the forest, do not follow them.
  • If a member of your group appears confused or disoriented, notify your ranger immediately.
  • If you hear your name called from the trees, inform your ranger but do not respond.
